YUVANIDHI PLUS

Yuvanidhi Plus

Yuvanidhi Plus is a Karnataka government initiative wherein the Yuva Nidhi scheme beneficiaries are offered skill-based training programs to equip them with skills make them emloyable. This skill based training program aims to enhance the job prospects of Yuva Nidhi beneficiaries by providing them with industry-relevant skills. The initiative aims to train 25,000 Yuva Nidhi beneficiaries under various skill courses.

Training Providers:

Yuvanidhi Plus utilizes government training institutions, including the Centre for Entrepreneurship Development of Karnataka (CEDOK), Government Tool Room and Training Centre (GTTC), Karnataka German Technical Training Institute (KGTTI), and the Vocational Training Providers of the Karnataka Skill Development Corporation under the Chief Minister’s Kaushalya Karnataka Scheme.

Yuvanidhi Plus Scheme

As announced in the Budget for the year 2024-25 and as per the Government Order No: KAUJIE 238 KAU-GUI 2024, Bengaluru, dated: 12.12.2024, the Department of Skill Development, Entrepreneurship, and Livelihood has approved the implementation of skill development programs under the Karnataka Skill Development Corporation/Skill Mission through the following government institutions:

  • KSDC
  • GTTC
  • Bharata Ratna Sir. M Visvesvaraya, National Training Facility for Skills for All (BMVNTFSA)
  • CEDOK
  • Karnataka ITI Employment Society (KIUS)
  • KGTTI (KGMSDC)

The objective is to provide short-term skill training and entrepreneurship training in various job roles under the Yuvanidhi Plus Scheme. A total target of 7,387 candidates registered under the scheme will be trained by the above government organisations.
